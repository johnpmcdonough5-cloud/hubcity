About Us

Farm Supply the Way It Should Be

Hub City Livestock Supply LLC is a locally owned farm and livestock supply store serving Oelwein and the surrounding Eastern Iowa communities.

We carry the everyday essentials farmers and hobbyists count on — quality feed, sturdy fencing, animal health products, and honest service from people who know livestock. Stop by the store or send us a message; we're happy to help you find exactly what your operation needs.

Why folks shop with us

  • Straight answers from people who raise livestock themselves
  • Stocked for cattle, hogs, poultry, goats, and horses
  • Fair prices without the big-box runaround
  • Easy in, easy out — right on N Frederick Ave
